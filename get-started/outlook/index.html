Steps to Import to Outlook:

  • Instructions for Outlook Calendar:
    1. On your work laptop, open your Outlook email
    2. Find the email where you recieved the BHS_Calendar_2016_Yourname.ics file. It should show an attachment like this one:
    3. Save that file to a temporary location (for example your desktop):
    4. At the top-left of your email client, click on File:
    5. Select Open and Export and then Open Calendar.. Then select the BHS_Calendar_2016_Yourname.ics file you just downloaded.
    6. Your calendar should load and appear side by side with your default calendar.
    7. To get a better view, change a few settings:
      • Make sure your timezone is Bermuda
      • Click on the left arrow to expand the calendar
      • Change from monthly view to "work week"
    8. Congratulations! You're ready!
